Interview prep
is broken.

Traditional interview prep gives you a list of questions and lets you rehearse scripted answers in front of a mirror. But real interviews aren't scripted. The interviewer has a personality. They have pet peeves. They have follow-up questions you didn't anticipate. They go quiet when you expected encouragement. They push harder when you thought you nailed it.


You can memorize every STAR answer in the book. But if you've never faced a skeptic who doubts every claim, a griller who won't let you stay surface level, or a silent judge who gives you nothing to read — you're not ready.


UnderFireAI gives you the pressure, unpredictability, and personality of a real interview. Because the best way to prepare for fire is to train in it.

"The backstory you never read becomes the interviewer you never expected."

Six Hidden Archetypes

You don't choose your interviewer. They choose you.

The Skeptic
"That's interesting, but can you give me a specific example?"
Doubts everything. Wants proof, metrics, and specifics. Vague answers trigger deeper probing.
